The normal voltage for a panel in this system is 39.0 volts and the normal current is 7.5 amps.

Fault Categories:

1. OPEN_CIRCUIT
   - Trigger: Panel power output near zero while irradiance is sufficient
   - Thresholds:
     - Po_mean < 0.02 * P_proj
     - Irradiance_mean > 400 W/m²

2. SNAPPED_DIODE
   - Trigger: Voltage ratios indicate missing diode contribution
   - Thresholds:
     - Vo_mean deviates by ~3.5% steps from inverter voltage
     - Proportional to number of diodes (1, 2, or 3 typically)

3. POWER_DROP
   - Trigger: Significant loss in panel output compared to expectations
   - Thresholds:
     - Po_mean or percent_loss < 95%
     - Not an open circuit or snapped diode

4. DEAD_PANEL
   - Trigger: Avg panel voltage is too low
   - Thresholds:
     - Avg_voltage < 1.5 V

5. LOW_VOLTAGE
   - Trigger: Panel/system voltage is below operating range
   - Thresholds:
     - Voltage < 20 V

6. LOW_POWER
   - Trigger: Low average power output
   - Thresholds:
     - Power < 10 W

7. GROSS_POWER_DROP
   - Trigger: Severe drop in output
   - Thresholds:
     - Power loss > 50%
     - Often due to shade or weather

8. SHADING
   - Trigger: Intermittent or clustered power drop
   - Contextual

9. INVERTER_OFFLINE / STRING_OFFLINE
   - Trigger: Loss of data or link with inverter or string node
   - Contextual / Missing heartbeats
